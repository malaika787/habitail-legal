Frequently Asked Questions

Tap the Pets tab at the bottom, then tap + Add Pet. Enter your pet's name, species, and optionally a birthday. Your first pet is always free.
From the Today screen, tap the + button and select Add Habit. Choose a habit type, name, frequency, and optional reminder time. Habits will appear on the Today screen on scheduled days.
A streak increases each day you complete all scheduled habits for a pet. Days with no habits scheduled are rest days and won't break your streak. Missing a day that has habits will reset the streak to zero.
From the Today screen, tap the + button and select Log Symptom. Select symptom tags, set a severity level, and add any notes. You can also tap All good to log a healthy day.
Go to your pet's profile and tap Vet Report. Choose a time range and tap Export PDF to share the report with your vet. PDF export requires Habitail Pro.
Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. Your Pro subscription will be restored automatically.
Go to Settings → Manage Subscription to manage or cancel your Habitail Pro subscription. You can also cancel directly through iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
Go to Settings → Delete Account. This will permanently delete your account and all associated data. This action cannot be undone. Make sure to cancel any active subscriptions first.

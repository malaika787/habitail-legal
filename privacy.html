01 Information We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide Habitail's features:

  • Account information — your email address when you sign up
  • Pet data — pet names, species, birthdate, and photos you choose to add
  • Habit data — habits, routines, and completion logs you create
  • Symptom data — health symptoms and notes you log for your pets
  • Usage data — basic app usage information to improve the product

We do not collect your location, contacts, or any data from your device beyond what you explicitly enter into the app.

02 How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide and improve Habitail:

  • To sync your pets, habits, and symptoms across devices
  • To send habit reminders and push notifications you've enabled
  • To process your subscription through RevenueCat
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To improve the app's features and performance

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third parties for marketing purposes.

03 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and encrypted at rest.

Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using your operating system's secure storage (iOS Keychain).

While we take every reasonable precaution, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password for your account.

04 Third Party Services

Habitail uses the following third party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (supabase.com/privacy)
  • RevenueCat — subscription and purchase management (revenuecat.com/privacy)
  • Apple App Store — app distribution and in-app purchases

These services receive only the minimum data needed to perform their function. We do not use any advertising SDKs or analytics that track you across other apps.

05 Children's Privacy

Habitail is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the address below and we will delete that information promptly.

06 Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access — you can view all your data inside the app at any time
  • Export — Premium users can export symptom history as CSV
  • Delete — you can delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Delete Account
  • Correction — you can edit any data you've entered at any time

For requests that can't be handled in-app, contact us at the address below and we'll respond within 30 days.

07 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you have an active account. If you delete your account, all personal data — including your pets, habits, and symptom logs — is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Anonymized, aggregated usage statistics that cannot be linked to you may be retained indefinitely to improve the product.

08 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we'll notify you via email or an in-app notice before the changes take effect.

The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of Habitail after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
